Your rights

  • Right to withdraw consent - At any time, you have to right to withdraw any given privacy consent in this website
  • Right of access - You have the right to know how your data is being processed which is explained in this privacy policy
  • Right of rectification - You have the right to verify if the collected data about you is correct and if not, you have the right to ask for rectification to change your data to make it correct
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten) - Under some circumstances, you have the right to erasure of all of your personal data
  • Right to restriction of processing - Under some circumstances you have to right to restrict processing of your data
  • Right to data portability - if your data is collected automatically with your consent, you have the right to transfer your data to another controller
  • Right to object - if your data is collected by means other than consent, you have the right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to lodge a complaint - You have the right to complain with your supervisory authority

How to exercise those rights

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, contact me through the contact information at the top of the page. Any requests will be free of charge and handled within one month.
